Frequently asked questions

What people ask before building in Patna.

Is Vishwanath Construction GST registered?

Yes. Vishwanath Construction is a GST-registered proprietorship (GSTIN 10EUFPK3451L1ZQ), registered since March 2019 and based in Khajpura, Patna.

Which areas do you serve?

We build primarily in Patna and Danapur, with project experience across other parts of Bihar as well.

How is the construction cost calculated?

Cost depends on built-up area, number of floors, work type, material mode, and package (Basic, Standard, or Premium). Use our free calculator above for an instant estimate.

What materials do you use?

Material brand and specification vary by package — for example, Standard and Premium packages use brands like ULTRATECH cement, TATA steel, HAVELLS wiring, and JAQUAR fittings. Full specs are listed in every quotation.
