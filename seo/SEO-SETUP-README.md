# SEO Setup — "Vishwanath Construction" search me top par aane ke liye

## Website me kya kya fix kiya gaya (already done)

1. **robots.txt aur sitemap.xml ab sahi jagah hain** — pehle ye `seo/` folder ke andar dabe hue the, jahan Google unhe dhoondh hi nahi sakta tha. Ab dono site ke root me hain (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`) jahan har search engine automatically check karta hai.

2. **Admin/Dashboard ab search me nahi aayenge** — `robots.txt` aur `noindex` meta tag se block kiya, taaki customer ko Google pe aapka admin panel na dikhe.

3. **Har page ka apna Title aur Description** — pehle kai pages (calculator, contact, projects) me ye missing tha. Ab har page Google search results me alag, sahi tarike se dikhega.

4. **GST aur Patna/Danapur location har jagah** — title, description, aur Schema (structured data) me GSTIN 10EUFPK3451L1ZQ, Khajpura address, aur Patna+Danapur dono add kiye gaye, taaki Google samjhe ki ye ek real, local, registered business hai.

5. **Structured Data (Schema) fix kiya** — purana `schema.json` file me ek typo tha jisse poora schema invalid ho gaya tha (Google use ignore kar raha tha). Ab valid hai, aur isme business details, services, aur FAQ — sab structured format me hai jisse Google search results me extra info (jaise FAQ dropdown) dikha sakta hai.

6. **Har page pe ab proper navigation hai** — pehle About, Contact, Projects, Blog, Quotation, aur teeno Calculator pages pe header/footer hi nahi tha — sirf "Back to home" link tha. Isse Google ko site ka structure samajhna mushkil hota tha, aur customer bhi confuse hota tha. Ab har page se har dusre page tak link hai.

7. **Footer me Calculator tools ka link add kiya** — pehle ye 3 useful pages (House Cost, Material, EMI calculator) kahi se bhi link nahi the, isliye Google unhe dhoondh hi nahi pata tha.
