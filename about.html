Our Story

Started with one belief —
honest construction.

Vishwanath Construction was founded with a simple belief: homeowners in Bihar deserve the same construction transparency that large corporate projects get. Every stage should be visible, every rupee trackable, every decision explained.

Since 2014, we have built homes for families across Patna and Danapur — and every single project has been billed stage-by-stage, with engineer sign-off, with verified materials, and with zero hidden charges.
