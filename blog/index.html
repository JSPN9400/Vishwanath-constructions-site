What should families check before selecting a construction company?

Choosing a builder in Patna is not just about getting the lowest quoted number. The stronger decision is to compare how clearly each company explains scope, pricing, execution quality, and reporting.

1. Transparent quotation structure

  • The quotation should clearly separate structure work, finishing work, or full turnkey delivery.
  • Material mode should be explicitly stated as with material or without material.
  • GST number, billing structure, and exclusions should be visible.

2. Site supervision and reporting

  • Ask how often you will receive site updates and who is responsible for communication.
  • Professional builders should explain stage checks for RCC, masonry, waterproofing, finishing, and handover quality.

3. Material quality discussion

  • Material brands, specifications, and quantity assumptions should be discussed before execution pressure starts.
  • Without this clarity, even a good starting rate can become expensive later.

4. Timeline and milestone discipline

  • Construction planning should include milestone-based payments and time expectations.
  • Good companies talk about process, not just price.
